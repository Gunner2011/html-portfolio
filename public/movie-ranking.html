Pieces of Media That Andrei Loves!

A short list of movies and shows Andrei thinks are great.

1) My Way (2011)

I usually don't care about historic movies, but this film is not only about the action. It focuses on two characters who are enemies from childhood and how their rivalry is forged into friendship through the hardships of war.
~ P.S. I cried at the end :'(

2) Cyberpunk Edgerunners

This is my number one anime of all time. The show wraps up in one season because it is paced well and knows exactly what story it wants to tell. Plus the setting is from a video game I also love.

3) Joker (2019)

You might remember the wave of controversy this film made back when it was released. It was either praised for it's critique of society, or hated for it's inspiration of violence. Nonetheless, I love it because it finally explains the Joker's backstory from Batman.
